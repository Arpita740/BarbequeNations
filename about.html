Welcome to Barbeque Nation!

One of the leading casual dining chains in India, Barbeque Nation pioneered the concept of “over the table barbeque” live grills embedded in dining tables – allowing guests to grill their own barbecue’s right at their tables. Barbeque Nation was founded in 2006 with the concept of 'all you can eat'.
